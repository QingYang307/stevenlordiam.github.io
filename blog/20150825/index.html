Introducing Leet 1.2 for iOS

Thanks for all your support for Leet. I received many feedbacks and I had put all your feature request in my developing plan. So last weekend I spent one day developing some highly requested features.

First thing you would love, all solutions for Leetcode! After I launched this app, Jiuzhang contacted me and gave me credit to use their solutions. So I wrote a web crawler in Python to get all the solutions. I also use “Show/Hide Answer” button in my app to show/hide the solutions, of course, codes are syntax highlighted.

Second, I have made reviewing the problems and answers easier by supporting landscape mode. You can now rotate your iPhone or iPad to review them in a much wider way.

Also, I have added notification for you to remind you to “shua-ti” everyday, now you will never miss a day to review leetcode problems.

Please rate it if you think Leet is helpful. Also if you find any bug or have any advice, send me an email in this app. I appreciate it.
